## Exam intelligence (Feb 2026 workbook)

- Format: 80 MCQs (1 mark) + 5 caselets x 4 questions (20 marks) = 100 marks, 2 hours, pass 60%, negative marking 0.25 per wrong answer.
- Roughly 35% theory, 35% numericals (40+ calculation questions), 30% case studies.
- Heaviest chapters: Technical Analysis (15), Financial Analysis (12), Valuation (12), Legal/Regulatory (10). Ch8 + Ch10 = 24 marks and are the most numerical.
- Test centres provide Excel / LibreOffice Calc for numerical computation.
- NISM does not publish past papers; questions here are original, written to match the workbook's style, illustrations, and chapter-end samples.
